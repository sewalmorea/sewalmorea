Who We Are

A voice for
sacred tradition

The Morea Sewalate is an advocacy organisation working toward the legitimisation of psychoactive plants and substances in their spiritual and ceremonial contexts. We believe in the sovereignty of individuals and communities to engage in practices rooted in millennia of human experience.
